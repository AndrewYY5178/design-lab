Design Principles

✅ Do❌ Don't
Full-serif stack for brand pagesSans-serif for main brand headings
B&W as bone structure + 1 accent colorPurple gradients, neon palettes
Noise texture overlay on dark backgroundsPure flat colors without texture
Hover → indigo shift (≥ 0.5s transition)Instant color changes, aggressive animations
Single-file HTML, zero npm dependenciesReact/Vue frameworks for brand pages
Particles as narrative (9 forms: dream/walk/flow...)Decorative particles without meaning
